## Why Get a Pre-Purchase Inspection?

Buying a property is one of the biggest financial decisions you'll make. A professional building inspection helps you:

- **Identify hidden defects** that could cost thousands to repair
- **Negotiate a fair price** based on the property's true condition
- **Avoid costly surprises** after settlement
- **Make an informed decision** about your investment

## What We Inspect

Our comprehensive pre-purchase inspections cover:

### Structural Elements
- Foundation and footings
- Load-bearing walls
- Roof structure and framing
- Floor and ceiling structures

### Building Exterior
- Roof covering and gutters
- External walls and cladding
- Windows and doors
- Drainage and site conditions

### Interior
- Wall and ceiling linings
- Floor coverings
- Built-in fixtures
- Moisture and ventilation

### Services
- Plumbing (visible areas)
- Electrical (visual inspection)
- Hot water systems
- Heating and cooling
